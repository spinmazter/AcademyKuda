Biography

Kudakwashe Mhurure, born in 1981, is a highly experienced table tennis coach with a passion for the sport that spans two decades. He began his coaching career in 2000 and has since become ITTF Level One certified, equipping him with the skills to train athletes at various levels.

In his quest to promote table tennis and develop emerging talent, Kudakwashe founded Spinmasterz Table Tennis Academy. The academy is dedicated to providing high-quality training, fostering a love for the game, and helping players reach their full potential.
